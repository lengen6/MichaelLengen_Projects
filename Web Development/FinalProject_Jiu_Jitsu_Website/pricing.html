Pricing

Contact Us

We offer several packages to our students based on how many classes they would like/are able to take each week. Each price is per month with no contract required. Prepurchase 6 months up front and receive a 10% discount and prepurchase 12 months and receive a 15% discount. In addition to the number of classes listed under each package all current members may attend open mats and use the strength and conditioning facilities without any further charge. Private lessons are available upon request and pricing/scheduling info can be obtained by speaking with the coaches directly.

Youngling: 1 Class per week - $50

Padawan: 2 Classes per week - $70

Knight: 3 Classes per week - $90

Master: Unlimited classes per week - $115

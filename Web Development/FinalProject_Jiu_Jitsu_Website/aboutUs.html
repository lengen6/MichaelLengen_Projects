About Us

The Mats

Our facility is housed in a converted warehouse split into two sections. One for mats and another for weights/cardio. Our mats area is quite spacious with enough space for 20 students at one time. This includes enough room to safely conduct live takedowns and sparring with the full 20 students. The mats are thick to ensure safety and cleaned before and after each practice to ensure cleanliness. We also conduct monthly inspections to guarantee that they are in perfect working order and when they are not we replace them promptly.

The Weights

In addition to the mat area where our classes are conducted, we also have a full strength and conditioning section. We believe that while technique is paramount any martial endeavor, by definition, requires a strong and healthy body to be fully effective. Towards this end, we have a full weight room with squat racks, benches, free weights, spinning bikes, rowing machines, sleds, and many other pieces of fitness equipment. We also have staff that are knowledgeable and can instruct students in the proper and effective use of our equipment.

The Location

We are located just of the Elyria/Sheffield exit of I-90 on Detroit Rd. nearby Lorain County Community College and the surrounding shopping area. We have found that this location is convenient for many of the residents of Lorain County. We draw many students from the college as well as professionals who live or work in the area.

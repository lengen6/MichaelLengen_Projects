Coaches

Matt House

Matt was an All-American collegiate wrestler at The Ohio State University. After completing his degree he found Jiu Jitsu and immediately fell in love with the sport. He is a brown belt and has been training Jiu Jitsu for the past 7 years. He specializes in the standup game and quick and efficient takedowns. When you roll with Matt or any of his proteges you need to be on your toes or you will soon find yourself on your back.

Steven Sitkowski

Steven is our resident black belt and has been training Jiu Jitsu for 15 years, 5 of those years were with us. He received his black belt under John Danaher in NYC before moving back to his hometown of Elyria. He is an accomplished competitor whose accolades include 7 gold medals from IBJJF events, 10 Pan-American golds, as well as a whole host of other medals throughout his career. He is known for his impressive guard and being able to make the best out of bad situations. He is also an enthusiastic yoga practitioner and will often end a hard training session by leading students through basic yogic positions.

Bob Robertson

Bob “Squared” Robertson began his martial arts journey at 12 years old with Kodokan Judo, eventually receiving his black belt at age 20. His friend introduced him to Jiu Jitsu 4 years ago and he has never looked back, now the proud holder of a purple belt. His Gi throws, takedowns, and grip fighting are second to none and he enjoys passing his skills on to a new generation. Outside of the gym Bob enjoys painting and competing in triathlons.

Roy Ares

Roy began training with us 6 years ago as a misty-eyed white belt. He has achieved the rank of brown belt and now teaches with us part time. Roy also has a background in competitive powerlifting and is accordingly responsible for overseeing our strength and conditioning program in addition to his time on the mats. His style emphasizes strength and explosiveness as a complement to technique. Whether you need help with an armbar or want to know the best program to add to your bench press Roy is your guy.
